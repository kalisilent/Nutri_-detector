## 2. What We Collect

### Provided by you
- **Email address** — required to create an account and log in
- **Name** — optional, used to personalize the app
- **Password** — stored only as a salted bcrypt hash; we never see the plaintext

### Generated by your use of the app
- **Photos of food packets** — used only to extract text via OCR; stored encrypted
  if you save the scan, deletable per-scan from the History screen
- **Scan results** — ingredients, additives, grades, raw OCR text — tied to your account
- **Device & diagnostic data** — through Firebase Crashlytics: device model, OS version,
  stack traces (no personally identifiable information)
- **Usage events** — through Firebase Analytics: which screens you visit, how often you
  scan, button taps. Anonymous and aggregate.

### We do NOT collect
- Location
- Contacts
- Microphone or call data
- Financial information
- Browsing history outside the app

## 3. How We Use Your Data

| Purpose | Data used |
|---|---|
| Provide the scanning service | email, password hash, scan photos, scan results |
| Show your scan history & dashboard | scan results |
| Improve OCR accuracy and add new ingredients | anonymized OCR failure samples (with consent) |
| Diagnose crashes and bugs | crash reports |
| Understand which features are used | anonymous analytics events |

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** show advertisements.
We do **not** share your data with third parties for marketing.

## 4. Where Data Is Stored

- Account and scan data → Google Cloud SQL (PostgreSQL), region [REGION]
- Scan images → Google Cloud Storage, region [REGION]
- Crash reports → Firebase Crashlytics (Google LLC)
- Analytics → Firebase Analytics (Google LLC)

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest.

## 5. How Long We Keep Data

- **Account data** — until you delete the account
- **Scan history** — until you delete the scan, or 24 months of inactivity
- **Uploaded photos** — same as the associated scan
- **Crash logs** — 90 days

## 6. Your Rights

You can, at any time:
- **Access** your data — request via [privacy@yourdomain.com]
- **Delete** your account — via the Profile screen or by email request
- **Delete individual scans** — via the History screen
- **Export** your data in JSON format — request via email
- **Withdraw consent** for analytics — in your device's app settings

If you live in the **EU/UK** (GDPR), the **California** (CCPA), or any
jurisdiction with similar laws, you also have the right to lodge a complaint
with your local data protection authority.

## 7. Children

NutriScan is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
data from children. If you believe we have, contact us and we'll delete it.

## 8. Security

We use industry-standard measures: HTTPS, encrypted databases, hashed passwords
(bcrypt), least-privilege access controls, automated dependency scanning.
No system is perfectly secure, but we work continuously to protect your data.
